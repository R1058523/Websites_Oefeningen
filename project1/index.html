About me

Welcome to my personal webpage! I'm Kilian, a 30-year-old with a unique background shaped by both hands-on craftsmanship and a growing passion for technology. I began my career working alongside my father in our family-owned bakery, where I focused on the craft of baking rather than managing the business. Unfortunately, the bakery had to close due to the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic, which led me to seek new opportunities. I transitioned to working as a manager at Domino's Pizza, where I gained valuable experience in team leadership and customer service.

Though my past has been rooted in food service, my true passion lies in the IT sector, particularly in programming. I'm new to the world of coding, and while I don't have much experience yet, I'm eager to learn and grow in this field. This website is a reflection of my journey as I step into the world of technology, and I’m excited to share my progress and future projects.

Thanks for visiting, and feel free to follow along as I continue to build my skills and explore the endless possibilities in programming!
